Reasons I like Demon
            Slayer

  1. The Story

    1. The story is enticing,
      it draws you in and you
      become completely invested
      in the character's journeys

    2. It makes you feel the feels, it's
      full of extrememly emotional
      moments.

  2. The Animation

    1. The Animation is stunning,
      making every shot look like
      a piece of art! The artistic use of
      different elements to highten the fights,
      and different styles used make the whole
      show a delight to watch visually.

  3. The Worldbuilding

    1. Has a unique twist of historic Japan.

    2. The world is very thought out, the
      rules of the world are well established,
      and it's expansive with a lot of lore.
