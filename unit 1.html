Led-Zeppelin

Why I like them?

I'm a little biased I think, one of my grandpas is the drummer in a Led-Zeppelin tribute band. I'm a fan of classic rock in general but I love Zeppelin in particuler because of all of the storytelling they do in their music. Robert Plant the lead singer was a big Tolkien fan, and he often put refernces to the books in his songs, and drew inspiration from the fantasy genre often. One of my favorite songs Stairway to Heaven is often described as the hero's journey in the form of a song.

Who were they?

The members of Led-Zeppelin were:

  • Robert Plant - Singer
  • Jimmy Page - Lead Guitar
  • John Paul Jones - Bass Guitar
  • John Bonham - Drummer
